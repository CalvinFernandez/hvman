About

     Hvman is a non-traditional encyclopedia that seeks to define the world as experienced by us, humans.

Hvman is a supplement to the scientific definitions found in encyclopedias and strives to define things empathically.

How does it work?

Hvman mirrors Wikipedia. If the article exists on Wikipedia, then the topic exists here.

But, that doesn't guarantee that content exists for that subject. That's where you come in. If you've experienced it, write about it and contribute your experience to a more perfect human definition of the subject.

What goes here?

Eloquent and powerful stories. No scientific definitions, please.
